AI tab — which content layout?

The Positions and Activity tabs are tabular. The AI tab is the new idea — what shape should it take?

A

Narrative Summary

One hero block of generated prose: "Your portfolio is concentrated in stablecoins on Base, with a moderate borrow position against ETH collateral. Health factor is healthy at 2.1…" Reads like an analyst memo. Minimal structure.

B

Structured Risk Cards

Grid of cards: Overall Risk Score, Concentration Risk, Liquidation Risk, Yield Quality, Suggested Actions. Each card has a metric + 2-3 sentences of AI-generated explanation. Scannable, dashboard-feel.

C

Hybrid: Summary + Risk Cards + Chat

Hero narrative summary at top, structured risk cards in the middle, and a chat input at the bottom ("Ask about your positions…"). Shows both the proactive analysis and the interactive Q&A potential.

D

Chat-First

The whole tab is a chat interface, with one starter message from the AI summarizing the wallet, plus suggested follow-up prompts ("What's my biggest risk?", "How could I improve yield?"). Minimal scaffolding around it.
